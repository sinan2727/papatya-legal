# Papatyam Legal Documents

Public hosting for Privacy Policy and Terms of Service of the Papatyam mobile app.

The main application repository is private; this repo exists solely for
GitHub Pages hosting of legal documents (required by Google Play Store and
Apple App Store).

## Live URLs

- Index: https://sinan2727.github.io/papatya-legal/
- Privacy Policy: https://sinan2727.github.io/papatya-legal/privacy_policy
- Terms of Service: https://sinan2727.github.io/papatya-legal/terms_of_service
